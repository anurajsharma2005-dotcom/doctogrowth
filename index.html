FAQ

Common questions

What does "results guaranteed" actually mean?
Before we start, we agree on specific performance targets — views, engagement, enquiry growth. If we don't hit those targets within the first 90 days, we either keep working for free until we do, or refund your money in full. We put our fees on the line because we know the system works.
How much time does this take from me?
About 1–2 hours per month. We send you topics and talking points ahead of time. You show up, talk through them on camera, and we handle everything else — editing, writing, design, publishing.
Do you only work with doctors?
Yes. We specialise exclusively in specialist medical practitioners — dermatologists, cardiologists, surgeons, and other consultants in the UK, US, and Middle East.
Can I approve content before it goes live?
Absolutely. You get full approval on everything before it's published. We use a simple review system — usually takes about 5 minutes to review and approve.
What platforms do you cover?
YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Facebook, blog/SEO, email newsletters, and podcast clips. We go wherever your patients are.
Do you offer services beyond content?
Yes — we also build patient acquisition funnels, AI automation systems, CRM setups, and conversion-optimised websites. These can be discussed on your strategy call.
Is the content HIPAA & GDPR compliant?
Non-negotiable. All content is reviewed for medical accuracy and regulatory compliance before publishing. We understand the stakes in healthcare.
